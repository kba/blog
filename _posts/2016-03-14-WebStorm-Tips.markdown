# Auto complete

Instead of write     
	
	<md-content></md-content>
, try to insert
	
	md-content 
 
then press TAB, you will see by magic 

    <md-content></md-content>

Advanced usage is 
	
	md-content#content

Then TAB, you will get 

    <md-content id="content"></md-content>

And with "." you can get 

	md-content.content

    <md-content class="content"></md-content>
